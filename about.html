About FreeMoneyIQ

We built the finance tools we wished existed — honest, fast, and completely free.

Why We Built This

Personal finance decisions — buying a home, paying off debt, planning savings goals, setting a freelance rate — are some of the most important financial choices people make. Yet the tools available to help with those decisions are often paywalled, buried in lead-generation funnels, or designed to push you toward a product rather than inform your decision.

FreeMoneyIQ was built to be different. We created a set of straightforward, accurate calculators and plain-language guides that give you real answers based on your real situation — with no email required, no account, no sales pitch, and no data sold to financial services companies. You use the tool, get your answer, and walk away informed.

Our goal is simple: make financial clarity accessible to everyone, regardless of income, background, or financial sophistication. Whether you're buying your first home, figuring out how to escape debt, or planning a savings goal for the first time — these tools are for you.

How We Make Money

FreeMoneyIQ displays Google AdSense advertisements. These are standard display ads — not financial lead forms, not affiliate links to specific lenders. The ads shown are based on your browser's ad settings and are managed entirely by Google. We do not share your personal information to serve ads.

Some of our blog articles may contain affiliate links — when you click and purchase through those links, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. We only recommend tools and resources we genuinely believe are useful. Affiliate income does not influence our calculator results or editorial content.

This model lets us offer all calculators and guides permanently free with no paywalls, no required accounts, and no subscription fees.

Important Disclaimer

FreeMoneyIQ's calculators and articles provide estimates and general information only. They are educational tools, not financial advice. Actual mortgage rates, tax outcomes, investment returns, and financial results vary based on your individual circumstances, location, credit profile, and the specific financial institutions and products you use.

Before making any major financial decision — including taking on a mortgage, investing, or significant debt payoff strategy changes — consult with a qualified financial professional licensed in your state.
