About CalcSmart

CalcSmart is a free collection of finance calculators built to help everyday people understand their money — without signing up, paying, or sitting through a sales pitch.

We believe financial clarity shouldn't be gated behind expensive software or complicated spreadsheets. Whether you're buying a home, paying off debt, planning for retirement, or figuring out what to charge as a freelancer — our tools give you fast, accurate answers.

All calculations are performed in your browser. We don't collect or store any data you enter.

Disclaimer: CalcSmart is for informational purposes only. Results are estimates. Always consult a qualified financial professional before making major financial decisions.
